# Robotic Eye Project

A robotic eye system with servo-controlled movement and articulated eyelids for realistic eye tracking and blinking animations.

## Overview

This project creates a pair of robotic eyes that can move and blink realistically. The eyes are controlled by servo motors and built using 3D printed components.

## Bill of Materials (BOM)

### Electronics
- **Arduino board** × 1
- **Servo motors** × 6 (3 per eye)
- **Controller/Driver board** × 1
- **Arduino USB cable** × 1
- **Breadboard** × 1
- **Small switches** × 2
- **Jumper wires bundle** (male to male) × 1
- **Additional small wires** (for connections)

### 3D Printed Components (Per Eye)
- **Eyelid** × 2
- **Eyeball** × 2
- **Eye holder** × 2
- **Eye frame** × 2

### Hardware & Assembly
- **Metal rod (sariya)** × 1
- **Nuts and bolts (M6)** × 12 (6 per eye)

### Tools & Supplies
- **3D printer** (for printing components)
- **Hot glue gun** (small size)
- **Glue sticks**
- **Printed eye graphics** (for realistic appearance)
